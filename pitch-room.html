funder path

how to explain the company in four moves.

problem

People already sleep with audio, but normal earbuds are built for daytime: bulky shells, touch mistakes, bright phones, and pillow pressure.

product

YEMA-1 proves the iconic object. YEMA PRO proves side-sleeper comfort. YPOD Remote proves the ecosystem.

business

Sell earbuds, remotes, custom wraps, limited editions, uploaded designs, and eventually saved sleep routines.

next build

Prototype comfort, test controls, validate wrap demand, collect waitlist users, and pitch a product family rather than a one-off device.

phase 1

lock YEMA-1 geometry, brand language, ecommerce site, and waitlist.

phase 2

prototype YEMA PRO comfort for side sleepers and validate low-profile fit.

phase 3

ship YPOD Remote with dream button, wraps, and user-uploaded customization.

phase 4

turn bedtime presets, community wraps, and limited drops into recurring demand.
